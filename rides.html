Gravel Routes To-Do List

Being back in Europe opens the door to some incredible gravel cycling routes. Right outside my door here in Austria are endless farm roads, double track, single track, and hike-a-bike routes... all with stunning views and a pristine environment.

I spent several months designing two event parcours as Austria lacks a flagship gravel event. The first is a one day event based out of a picturesque forest district village with three different distance categories. The longest is 200 kilometers and climbs around 6000m elevation. A n epic course. The second is a 300km mini-ultra-gravel event starting in Krems. The parcours journeys through a nice balance of the incredible vineyards and hilly forests above the Danube.

Bringing these events to the public is what has inspired me to learn coding.

Aside from my home roads and projects, here are a few of the established events I hope to challenge myself on in the new year.
