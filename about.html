And what would be your dream setup?

I'm a luddite at heart so would like nothing more than to toss all these devices in a deep hole and be done with it. But I also love information, so here we are. The dream is to have a functioning remote setup, whether on foot, bicycle or motor vehicle, for seamless and efficient workflow and business organisation. Every detail considered. So I can be surfing in some remote part of Indonesia or on my bike in deep central Spain and know exactly what the state of my actionables are. And have total confidence in my hardware, power sources and security systems.

I developed a solid remote system over the past 5 years, which I had the privilege to test around the world in a variety of challenging environments and configurations. I am looking now to elevate my web dev skills to optimise the whole package.

<Rob Grace Resume> is a modest first step towards realising this goal.
